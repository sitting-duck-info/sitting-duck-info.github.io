The Sitting Duck Info Story

Published Sep 3, 2018 by Sitting Duck Info in Demo at https://sittingduck.info/about-us/

Many years ago, Sarah had a vision. She wanted the world to be more like a duck.

Along with her close friend, Chuck Hannah, she began building Sitting Duck Info.

Now the world is more like a duck.

Sarah Taylor is the founder and CTO of Sitting Duck Info, where she advocates for ducks and duck accessories. She likes to write about technology and wildlife.
